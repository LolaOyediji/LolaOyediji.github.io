Beyoncé Knowles' timeline:

  • 1981 - Beyonce Giselle Knowles was born in Houston, Texas.
  • 1997 - Destiny's Child release their first hit single 'No, No, No'.
  • 1998 - Destiny's Child release their self-titled album.
  • 2003 - Beyonce releases her debut solo album, 'Dangerously in Love'.
  • 2003 - Beyonce releases 'Crazy In Love' single.
  • 2004 - Destiny's Child release their forth and final studion album, 'Destiny Fulfilled'.
  • 2006 - Dreamgirls is released.
  • 2007 - Beyonce realeses the single 'Listen'.
  • 2008 - Beyonce and Jay-Z marry at private ceremony in New York City.
  • 2009 - Kanye West comandeers Taylor Swift's microphone during an acceptance speech at the MTV Video Music Awards.
  • 2010 - Beyonce takes home 6 grammy awards of the 10 she was nominated for - including song of the year for 'Single Ladies' and best female pop vocal for 'Halo' - becoming the only female artist to win six trophies in one night.
  • 2012 - Beyonce and Jay-z welcome a new baby girl to the world, Blue Ivy Carter in New York City.
  • 2013 - Beyonce releases her self-tiltled album. It debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 with three-day sales of 617,213 digital copies. Six days after release it had sold one million digital copies worldwide.
  • 2016 - Beyonce releases a new album, 'Lemonade'.
  • 2017 - Beyonce gives birth to twins, Rumi and Sir Carter.
  • 2018 - Beyonce and Jay-Z go on joint world tour, 'On The Run 2'. They also release joint album, 'Everything Is Love'.
  • 2018 - Beyonce becomes the first Black woman to headline Coachella.
  • 2019 - Beyonce releases her Netflix documentary, 'Homecoming'.
